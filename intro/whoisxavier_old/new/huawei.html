SVG Gradient Experiment

The whole concept for interactive color started with this little experiment. I wanted to capture the irdiscence quality of Huawei's phones, and to see what was possible with SVG in terms of masking, color and performance.

I turns out you can do a LOT. Making use of SVG's masking properties, I was able to use optimized jpg images as transparent, add gradients with blending modes that shift colors, and finally, make the phone interact with the mouse.
